CHAA receives sponsorship from Bay Area foundations and businesses in support of activities and events. Some of our sponsors include:

## [California Endowment](http://www.calendow.org)

![California Endowment](/images/CaliforniaEndowment.png)

The California Endowment has sponsored our Southeast Asian Youth Stopping Violence Summits in West Contra Costa County, and our 10 Year Anniversary Celebration in 2006.

## [California Wellness Foundation](https://www.calwellness.org/) 

![California Wellness Foundation](/images/CaliforniaWellnessFoundation.png)

The California Wellness Foundation is funding the capacity development of CHAA’s youth violence prevention efforts in West Contra Costa County.

## [Kaiser Community Foundation](http://kp.org)

![Kaiser](/images/Kaiser.png)

Kaiser Community Foundation has supported our annual Southeast Asian Youth Stopping Violence Summits in West Contra Costa County.

## [San Francisco Foundation](http://www.sff.org)

![Kaiser](/images/SFF.jpg)

CHAA has received sponsorship from The San Francisco Foundation for our annual Southeast Asian Youth Stopping Violence Summits in West Contra Costa County.

## [United Way of the Bay Area](http://www.uwba.org)

![UWBA](/images/uwba.jpg)

CHAA has received annual sponsorship from United Way of the Bay Area for our annual Southeast Asian Youth Stopping Violence Summits in West Contra Costa County.

## [Wells Fargo](http://www.wellsfargo.com)

![Wells Fargo](/images/WellsFargo.png)

Wells Fargo has sponsored our annual Southeast Asian Youth Stopping Violence Summits in West Contra Costa County for 5 years running, and has contributed additional resources that enhance CHAA’s ability to achieve our mission and vision for health and wellness in API communities living in the East Bay. Wells Fargo also sponsored for our 10 Year Anniversary in 2006.
