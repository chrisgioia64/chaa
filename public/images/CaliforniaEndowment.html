We want to see healthy communities where youth and adults thrive and all community members develop their full potential, thus achieving greater health equity for all Californians.

$4.1BTotal Assets With more than $4.1 Billion in assets, the California Endowment is the largest private health foundation in the state.
22,000Total Grants Awarded Since its inception, the Endowment has awarded more than 22,000 grants totaling over $2.9 billion to community-based organizations throughout California.
29Years Serving California Created in 1996, The California Endowment is a private non-profit, statewide foundation that works to make California a healthier place for all.
