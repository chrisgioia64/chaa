Community Health for Asian Americans (CHAA) is a non-profit organization committed to improving the quality of life for marginalized communities with special focus on Asian and Pacific Islander (API) communities in the Bay Area.

We pride ourselves on:

- Our 29-year history
- Increasing well-being, health equality, and community engagement
- Connecting individuals and families to culturally and linguistically competent behavioral health, youth development, and wellness services
- Advocating for reducing health disparities and promoting ethnic leadership in low-SES, underserved and new immigrant communities
- Having a staff that speaks 25 languages and dialects, including Spanish
